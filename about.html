a little about me

I’m a Senior Implementation Specialist based in Atlanta GA with a passion for solving problems streamlining digital workflows and guiding clients through seamless transitions. With 5+ years of experience in training onboarding and client success I specialize in translating complex systems into smooth efficient processes that work for both teams and end users.
My love for tech started in middle school and has grown into a skillset I now use to build websites create digital content and enhance user experiences. Along the way I’ve sharpened my skills through SheCodes boot camps and freelance projects gaining proficiency in HTML, CSS, JavaScript and React. For me coding isn’t about leaving implementation it’s about adding value. Whether building internal tools polishing digital touchpoints or giving clients that extra layer of support I see technology as a way to create connect and contribute. At heart I’m a people first problem solver who happens to love clean code and clever interfaces.
